Research

My research interesting is apply ML and DL techniques to real healthcare problems. Previously, I wa worked on Human Activity Recognition (HAR) and detect fall in elder people. Currently, I engaged on Non-Rigid Structure from Motion (NRSfM), 3D reconstruction, and deformable modeling in medical imaging, with a particular focus on Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF) and non-rigid registration. His research lies at the intersection of computer vision and computer graphics.
